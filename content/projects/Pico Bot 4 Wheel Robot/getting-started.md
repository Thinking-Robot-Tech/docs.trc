# 🤖 Pico Bot 4 Wheel Robot (ESP32 Edition)

Welcome to the **[PICO BOT 4 Wheel Robot with ESP32 Edition](https://thinkingrobot.in/products/pico-bot-4-wheel-robot-with-esp32-edition-thinking-robot-modular-robotics-kit)** guide!

This comprehensive modular robotics kit gets you started with building your own 4-wheel drive robot powered by the powerful ESP32 microcontroller. 

[![Pico Bot](https://res.cloudinary.com/drwys1ksu/image/upload/v1772909280/pico_bot_transp_uipkeg.png)](https://thinkingrobot.in/products/pico-bot-4-wheel-robot-with-esp32-edition-thinking-robot-modular-robotics-kit)

## What's in the Box?

- 4x DC Gear Motors and Wheels
- ESP32 Development Board
- Motor Driver module
- Chassis and mounting brackets
- Connecting wires and battery holder
- Screws and standoffs
- Detailed manual
