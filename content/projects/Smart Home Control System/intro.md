## ⚡ What is Electronics?

Electronics is the science of controlling electricity using components like:

- 🔹 Resistors  
- 🔹 Capacitors  
- 🔹 Transistors  
- 🔹 Microcontrollers  

👉 In simple words:

> Electronics = **Controlling electricity to create useful systems**

From mobile phones to smart homes — everything runs on electronics.

---

## 🤖 What is Robotics?

Robotics is where **electronics + programming + mechanics** come together.

A robot can:
- 👀 Sense (using sensors)  
- 🧠 Process (using controllers like ESP8266 / Arduino)  
- ⚙️ Act (using motors, relays, pumps)

## 🌐 What is IoT (Internet of Things)?

IoT allows devices to:

- 📡 Connect to the internet  
- 📲 Send & receive data  
- 🎮 Be controlled remotely  

💡 Example:
Control your plant watering system from your phone.

As you explore Thinking Robot projects, you will master:

The foundation of every circuit.

### 🌡️ 2. Sensors
Devices that read real-world data:

- Light sensors  

### 🧠 3. Microcontrollers

- ESP8266

### ⚙️ 4. Actuators
Things that perform actions:
- Motors  
- Pumps  
- Relays  
- LEDs
